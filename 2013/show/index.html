CONGRATULATIONS

Congratulations to all students with work accepted into this year's show. We will be displaying your work at the National Student Conference, April 12-13 in Dallas. You can purchase tickets to the conference here. Please follow the instructions below to ensure that your work is displayed in the show correctly.

BOOKS, VIDEOS, AND PACKAGING

All complete books, video entries, and packaging pieces that were mailed for judging have been kept and do not need to be re-sent. However, you will need to send a credit tag. See complete credit tag instructions below.

PORTFOLIOS

All bound portfolios that were mailed for judging have been kept and do not need to be re-sent. If your portfolio has been selected for the show and was sent in unbound for judging, you still must send in a bound portfolio book for display in the show. We will be placing portfolios on tables, and if yours is not bound, there is no guarantee that it will be included in the show. You will need to send a credit tag for your portfolio. See complete credit tag instructions below.

LOGOS

Submit logos with a brief description below on an 8" x 8", 1/4" black foam core board.

PRINT ADS

Print ads should be approximately 8.5" x 11" or 17" x 11" for a spread and mounted on 1/4" black foam core boards. Again, there is no guarantee that all of your work will be hung if it is oversized.

ALL OTHER WORK

All other work must be mounted on 1/4" black foam core boards no larger than 24" x 36". Single entries should fit on one board, series entries on no more than three boards, and portfolio entries on no more than four boards. If you submit too many boards or boards that are too large, there is no guarantee that all of your work will be hung. If you would like to ask for exceptions to the size limitations, please email Josh DeLoach.

ALL WORK MUST INCLUDE CREDIT TAGS

Credit tags should be submitted flush-mounted on 1/4" black foam core. These will be displayed at the show beside your work. Be sure your credit tag is secured to the back of your entry. We also ask that you print a second copy of your credit tag and attach it to the back of each piece (and every piece in a series) so there is no confusion. You can download credit tags here.

SHIPPING WORK

All work must be received by April 9th. There will be no deadline extension. You can mail or drop off work at RBMM:
National Student Show
ATTN: Dodie Kindred
RBMM
7007 Twin Hills
Suite 200
Dallas, Texas 75231

ANNOUNCING WINNERS

Again, we congratulate you on being included in one of the best shows we've ever had. There were over 1,200 pieces submitted form schools across the country and judging was very tough. We will announce winners at the gala Saturday evening, April 13. Join us there and find out if you and your classmates won your share of $15,000 in scholarships and prizes. Buy gala tickets here.
